# Sharly-Chess.com

Our integration with [Sharly-Chess.com](https://sharly-chess.com) allows you to create and manage your events online.

The platform brings together organisers, players and arbiters in a single, modern interface — handling everything from event creation and player registration to live results display.

## For Organisers

- **Event Management** — Create and manage chess events with multiple tournaments and flexible capacity limits.
- **Player Registration** — Simplified registration process with tariff support and duplicate player detection.
- **Payment Processing** — Secure payment handling via Stripe, with funds going directly to the organiser. Automatic billing and refund management included.
- **Player Database Search** — Integrated lookup for accurate player information retrieval.
- **Products & Options** — Sell merchandise, meal packages and additional offerings alongside tournament entries.
- **Team Access** — Grant all team members organisational access to distribute the workload.
- **Built-in Messaging** — Communicate directly with players via integrated messaging, accessible and shared by the entire organising team.
- **Live Pairings & Results** — Display pairings and results in real time during tournaments for players and spectators.

## For Players

- **Modern Interface** — Clean, responsive design for easy tournament discovery and browsing.
- **Easy Registration** — Quick access to purchase and register for upcoming events.
- **Location Notifications** — Be informed of new tournaments in your areas of interest so you never miss a local event.
- **Personalised Tournament View** — View your ranking and pairings during events organised with the arbitration software.

## For Arbiters

- **Easy Synchronisation** — Seamless integration with arbitration software.
- **Matching Pairings** — Downloaded pairings match the arbitration software exactly, with no discrepancies.
- **Complete Results Upload** — Upload comprehensive results with all necessary details.
- **Unlimited Tiebreaks** — No limits on tiebreak quantity or format; all variations are supported.
