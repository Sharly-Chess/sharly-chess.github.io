_Sharly Chess_ 5.0 brings **team tournaments** — the biggest new feature yet.

## Team tournaments

You can now run full **team events**, where players compete in teams, teams are paired against each other, and standings are kept in team scores. The release adds:

- four team **pairing systems** — Team Swiss, Team Round-Robin, Two-game team match and Molter;
- **team scoring** with match points and game points, and a set of team tie-breaks;
- team **documents and screens** — match sheets, the Berger grid, team rankings, and team check-in / match / ranking displays.

👉 See the new [Team events]({% link docs/team-tournaments/index.en.md %}) section to get started.

## Molter, for everyone

The **Molter system** is a fixed-table method for small team events over a few rounds — everyone plays every round, nobody sits out. It is named after Charles Molter, whose 1978 documents were turned into ready-to-use tables in 1991 by international arbiter Jean-Claude Templeur and distributed by the _Fédération Française des Échecs_; it has been little known outside France. _Sharly Chess_ now **generates Molter tables automatically** for any supported size, bringing it to organisers everywhere.

👉 Read [Molter tables]({% link docs/team-tournaments/molter-tables.en.md %}) to learn when and why to use it.

## French team competitions

For French events, the [_FFE_ plugin]({% link docs/plugins/france/ffe.en.md %}) adds rule sets for the official team competitions — the Jean-Claude Loubatière Cup, the Women's championship (N1F / N2F) and the Mixed Cup — along with the _Loubatière_ pairing sheet.
