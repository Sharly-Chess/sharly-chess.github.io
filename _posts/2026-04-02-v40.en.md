For once, the main innovations in version 4.0 go beyond the arbitration software: _Sharly Chess_ is now integrated with our own online registration platform.

## Registration Platform _Sharly-Chess.com_

This new platform is the result of several months of development and is already being used successfully by a number of clubs.

Unlike general-purpose payment solutions, _Sharly-Chess.com_ is built specifically for chess competitions.

Seamlessly integrated with the _Sharly Chess_ arbitration software, it simplifies the work of organizers and arbiters, saving valuable time when preparing events.

Players also benefit from a growing set of online features, including live results tracking and pairing notifications.

The platform is now live — you can explore it here:

- [Access the platform](https://events.sharly-chess.com/)
- [Explore its features](https://events.sharly-chess.com/fr/features)

## _Sharly Chess_ Arbitration Software

Version 4.0 of _Sharly Chess_ also introduces several features requested by the community, including:
- **Smoother navigation** with keyboard shortcuts;
- **Definition of round schedules**, exported to _Chess-Results.com_ and of course _Sharly-Chess.com_;
- **Improved automatic uploading** of pairings and results;
- **Flatpak packaging for Linux**: replacing AppImage with Flatpak improves compatibility with many more Linux distributions, which will delight arbiters who prefer using a free operating system.
