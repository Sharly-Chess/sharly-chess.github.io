Version 3.3 has been released!
This update focuses on three main areas:

## Streamlined UI

We've made a number of improvements to make the interface cleaner and more consistent:

* Default settings for tournaments, prizes, and screens have been moved out of the _Event_ settings and into their respective pages.
  This makes them easier to find when needed and reduces the complexity of the _Event_ configuration.
* All dialogs have been reviewed and made more compact, so more information fits on screen.
  This minimizes the need for scrolling and improves readability.
